Have a look at Lucene config, it's all there, including tests.  This filter will take a token
such as "foobar" and chop it up into n-grams (e.g. foobar -> fo oo ob ba ar would be a
set of bi-grams).  You can specify the n-gram size and even min and max n-gram size.
