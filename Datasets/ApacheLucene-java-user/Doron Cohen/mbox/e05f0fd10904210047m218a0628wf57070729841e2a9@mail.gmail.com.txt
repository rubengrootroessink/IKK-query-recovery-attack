SUBJECT: Re: Why is CustomScoreQuery limited to ValueSourceQuery type?
DATE: 21 Apr 2009

CustomScoreQuery expects the VSQs to have a score for document matching the
(main) subQuery - this does not hold for arbitrary queries.
