You can add phrase on the writer field.

I.e. with high boost of 3 and low boost of 2,
writing 'h' for 'heading' and 'w' for 'writer',
try this query:

h:sachin^3  d:tendulkar^3  w:sachin^2  w:tendulkar^2  w:"h:Sachin
Tendulkar"^6
