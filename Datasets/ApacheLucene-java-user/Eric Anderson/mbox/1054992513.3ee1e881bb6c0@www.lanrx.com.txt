Update your configuration.jsp file, to reflect the new location of the index.

Also, if you redeploy the war file, you're going to overwrite the 
configuration.jsp that you modified the index location, in causing the software 
to look in the /opt/lucene/index location again.

I wouldn't redeploy the war, just restart tomcat.
