In my case, I know the upper and lower limits of my field.
So it becomes easy for me to run such queries.

eg.

*>  *
RangeQuery(value, upperLimit, inc)

*<*
RangeQuery(lowerLimit, value, inc)

*!=*
Use the in this case.
eg:
-field:value
