> StandardAnalyzer());
>

>

It is working. Without escaping you would specify an ariadne BUT 1
query. It is the StandardAnalyzer which drops the '-'. Anyway,
if you've indexed with the StandardAnalyzer, you'll hardly find dashes
in your text.
