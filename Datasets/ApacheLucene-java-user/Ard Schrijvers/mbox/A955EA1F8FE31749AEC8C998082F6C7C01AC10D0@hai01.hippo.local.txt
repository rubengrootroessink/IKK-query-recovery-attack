SUBJECT: RE: Should the IndexSearcher be closed after very search completed
DATE: 9 Jul 2007

Closing the IndexSearcher is best only after a deleteDocuments with a reader or changes with
a writer.

For performance reasons, it is better to not close the IndexSearcher if not needed
